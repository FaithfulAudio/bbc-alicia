Our Story

About Our Church

A King James, Bible-preaching Baptist church in the heart of rural Bohol — gathered by grace, serving the Lord in Alicia, Bohol.

Our Pastor

Pastor Dodong Curit

Our church is shepherded by Pastor Dodong Curit, who labors faithfully to feed the flock with the Word of God and to reach the lost of Alicia and beyond with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

You are warmly invited to come and hear the Word preached, to meet our church family, and to see the love of Christ at work in our congregation.

Our History

Thirty-Nine Years of God's Faithfulness

For thirty-nine years the Lord has been faithful to His people here in Alicia, Bohol. From humble beginnings the Lord has built a congregation devoted to soulwinning, to the singing of the old hymns of the faith, and to the steadfast preaching of the Word of God.

In May of 2026 our church family gathered for our 39th Anniversary & Conference under the theme of Psalm 116 — “I will pay my vows unto the LORD now in the presence of all his people.” Through every season, the goodness of God has been the one constant of our story, and to Him alone belongs the glory for all that He has done.

What We Believe

Our Faith in Brief

We are an independent, King James Bible Baptist church. The following is a brief summary of the truths we hold dear.

  1. 1.The Bible. We believe the Bible is the inspired, preserved, and infallible Word of God, and we preach and teach from the Authorized (King James) Version.
  2. 2.One True God. We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  3. 3.The Lord Jesus Christ. We believe in His virgin birth, sinless life, His atoning death on the cross for our sins, His bodily resurrection, and His coming again.
  4. 4.Salvation. We believe salvation is the free gift of God, received by grace through faith in the Lord Jesus Christ alone — not by works — and that every believer is eternally secure in Him.
  5. 5.The Local Church. We believe in the New Testament local church, in baptism by immersion of believers, and in gathering faithfully to worship, to grow, and to carry the gospel to every soul.
  6. 6.The Great Commission. We believe every Christian is called to share the good news of Jesus Christ — that whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved.
