Our Ministries

There is a place for everyone in the family of God — for every age and every season of life.

For Every Age

Ways to Grow & Serve

From the youngest child to the seasoned saint, our church offers a place to learn the Word of God, to serve the Lord, and to belong to a loving church family.

Children · Young People · Adults

Sunday School

Bible classes for every age each Sunday at 9:00 AM — teaching the whole counsel of God from the youngest child to the oldest saint.

Worship

Music Ministry

Our choir and congregation lift up the old hymns of the faith, leading God's people to worship Him in song from the heart.

Youth

Young People

A lively ministry for teens and young adults — fellowship, fun, and a firm foundation in the Word of God for the next generation.

Women

Gracious Ladies

The women of our church encourage one another in the Lord, growing in grace and serving the congregation and community together.

Sports Outreach

Basketball Ministry

Reaching the men and youth of Alicia through the game they love — building friendships that open the door to the gospel.

Evangelism

Soulwinning & Outreach

We take the good news of Jesus Christ beyond our walls — into homes, the community, and the far barangays — that whosoever will may be saved.
