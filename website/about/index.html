Our Story

About Our Church

A King James, Bible-preaching Baptist church in the heart of rural Bohol — gathered by grace, serving the Lord in Alicia, Bohol.

Our Pastor

Pastor Dodong Curit

Our church is shepherded by Pastor Dodong Curit, who labors faithfully to feed the flock with the Word of God and to reach the lost of Alicia and beyond with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

You are warmly invited to come and hear the Word preached, to meet our church family, and to see the love of Christ at work in our congregation.

Our History

Thirty-Nine Years of God's Faithfulness

For thirty-nine years the Lord has been faithful to His people here in Alicia, Bohol. From humble beginnings the Lord has built a congregation devoted to soulwinning, to the singing of the old hymns of the faith, and to the steadfast preaching of the Word of God.

In May of 2026 our church family gathered for our 39th Anniversary & Conference under the theme of Psalm 116 — “I will pay my vows unto the LORD now in the presence of all his people.” Through every season, the goodness of God has been the one constant of our story, and to Him alone belongs the glory for all that He has done.
