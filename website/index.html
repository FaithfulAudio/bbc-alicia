Welcome

A Church That Has Heart for You

We are an independent, King James, Bible Baptist church in the heart of rural Bohol. For more than three decades the Lord has gathered His people here in Alicia for soulwinning, old-fashioned hymns, and the faithful preaching of the Word of God. Whoever you are, wherever you have been — there is a seat and a welcome for you.

In the Heart of Bohol

Old-Fashioned, Bible-Believing, and Glad to See You

You will find a friendly congregation, hearty singing, and Bible preaching you can understand and live by. We hold to the King James Bible and to the simple gospel of the Lord Jesus Christ — that He died for our sins, was buried, and rose again the third day.

Bring the whole family. Whether it is your first time in church or you have walked with the Lord for years, you are welcome at Bible Baptist Church Alicia.
